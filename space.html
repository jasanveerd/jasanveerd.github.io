Why Space?

My curiosity about space began in middle school when my teacher talked about the Cassini mission and shared updates on its discoveries. Learning about a spacecraft exploring another planet made space feel vast, unknown, and exciting.

During high school, watching SpaceX’s Demo-2 launch during the pandemic completely captivated me. Seeing astronauts launch from Earth for the first time in nearly a decade showed me how far human innovation has come—and how much farther we can still go.
